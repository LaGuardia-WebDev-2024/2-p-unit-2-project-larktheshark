The Land Of Ooo - Adventure Time

Here are all the reasons why you should visit the land of Ooo!

Locations

Finn and Jake's Treehouse

A willow tree made into a house, Finn and Jake's Treehouse is a lovely, homely family friendly place to visit. They usually aren't home due to their adventures, but it would be the ideal place to stay out of all the other locations.

Candy Kingdom

The Candy Kingdom is a state entirely made up of candy, with candy people as it's citizens! It is run by Princess Bubblegum, who founded the state hundreds of years ago. It's a wonderful place to go if you don't have a sweet tooth, as it can be quite distressing for the citizens to see. Some highlights would be the Rock Candy Mountains, and the Cotton Candy Forest.

Nightosphere

The Nightosphere is an alternate dimension in Ooo populated by many different kinds of demons. I would not reccomend this location. To get there, you:

  1. Draw a happy face on a surface.
  2. Douse the face in bug milk.
  3. Chant, "Maloso vobiscum et cum spiritum!"
Don't bring your kids.
